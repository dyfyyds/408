# 写作常用表达

> 高分句型与连接词。

---

## 开头句型

- It is widely acknowledged that...
- In recent years, there has been a growing concern about...
- When it comes to..., different people hold different views.

## 过渡句型

- On the one hand... On the other hand...
- Not only... but also...
- In addition...
- Furthermore...
- However...
- Nevertheless...

## 结尾句型

- Taking all these factors into account, we may conclude that...
- In conclusion...
- From what has been discussed above, we can draw the conclusion that...
